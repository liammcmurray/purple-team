Cookies

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet, or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to:

  • remember what messages you have been shown so we do not show them again
  • understand how you use the service so we can update and improve it
  • temporarily store information you enter so you can use the service

The cookies we use do not identify you.
