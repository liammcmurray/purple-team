Set case level

ALL information containing the HIGH HARM serious injury/death, serious exploitation, or activity with adverse impact on individuals, the public, communities, the Home Office
Examples of red cases:
  • Drugs (importing into country)
  • Loss of life
  • Murder
  • Terrorism/ Radicalisation/ National Security
  • War crimes
  • Exploitation Modern Slavery (including prostitution into the country)
  • Human Trafficking
  • Child abuse or Child(ren) at risk
  • Firearm and knife offences
  • Kidnap
  • Violent crime (excluding common assault)
  • Sexual offences
  • Threats to life, abuses and harassment
  • Care Home – Safeguarding/abuse concerns against patients or the staff (including physical, intimidation, working practices etc)
TIME SENSITIVE Information which indicates activity contrary to immigration law, which may contain elements of HARM
Examples of Amber cases:
  • Urgent time sensitive cases with no harm - e.g individual leaving the country in a week or attending the port/airports within the next few days
  • Drug offences (dealing/possession)
  • Facilitation- Organised Group or Individuals
  • Robbery/burglary
  • Assault – common
  • Control of prostitution – within UK
  • Driving Offences – e.g no licence or insurance
  • Breach of Deportation Order - OIU
  • Care Home – details of a Care Home/ Recruitment Agency providing illegal workers/false documents/ imposters
  • Harm/Criminal offence abroad - outstanding warrant
  • HMO - only if harm/abuse is mentioned
Information that indicates activity contrary to immigration law but does not contain elements of HARM outside of this
Examples of Green cases:
  • Illegal working
  • Student/working in breach of visa
  • Asylum support fraud
  • Facilitation – local e.g family members
  • Clandestine entry
  • Deception to gain leave to remain
  • Forgery/identity card offences
  • Overstayer
  • Previous criminal convictions overseas (other than those listed)
  • Prostitution (individual – no control indicators noted)
  • Care Home – Overstayer/incorrect visa type/WIB
  • Sponsorship Abuse
  • HMO’s
Non immigration related information, no high harm
Examples of White cases:
  • Non-immigration criminality such as;
  • Tax evasion
  • Benefit abuse
  • Money Laundering
